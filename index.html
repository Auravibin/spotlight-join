What exactly is the Spotlight Membership? +
Spotlight is a monthly membership that gives you a complete identity-to-content operating system. Live calls twice a month, the Spotlight Hub, core training videos, and community — all built around three pillars: Identity, Brand, and Content.
When are the live calls? +
Every second Wednesday at 11AM EST. New Moon calls: identity intention, content direction, Operant Power activation. Full Moon calls: visibility audit, messaging refinement, offer clarity. These are live working sessions — not webinars.
What is the Spotlight Hub? +
A private members-only app you access after joining. Inside you can generate AI-powered content in your voice, save posts and strategy to your vault, track wins and testimonials, plan by moon cycle, build your brand foundation, and more. Everything syncs to your account on any device.
Where do replays and community communication happen? +
Everything happens in the Spotlight Telegram. Call links are dropped there before each session, replays are shared there after, and it's where we text-communicate between calls — accountability, feedback, wins, and real-time support from Aura and the group.
Is this for beginners or established creators? +
Both — but specifically for women who already know they have something powerful to offer and can't quite activate it consistently. If identity clarity and content confidence are the gap, this is where you close it.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Cancel anytime from your Stripe customer portal — no questions, no penalties. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.
What's the difference between Spotlight and Diamond Mentorship? +
Spotlight is the membership — group calls, the Hub, training library, Telegram community. Diamond Mentorship is Aura's high-ticket 1:many coaching container for women ready to go deeper with a more intimate, high-touch experience. Many Diamond clients are also Spotlight members.
